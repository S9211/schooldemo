Student Council

 

There is a Student Council, which consists of the staff & elected representives of the sections. The Dean Student Welfare (DSW) will be the Professor incharge of the Student Council. The Student Council consists of President, Vice-President, Secretary and the required no. of executive Members. DSW will arrange the meeting & keep the minutes. The Principal will be the chairman of the council. The Council will suggest to the Principal about various matters pertaining to the welfare of the Students.
