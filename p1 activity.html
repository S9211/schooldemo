Activities

The students of Nursery to Grade-II are offered a variety of activites. However the activities become more diverse from Grade-III onwards. From Grade-III onwards the students are divided into houses-

  1. Red House
  2. Blue House
  3. Yellow House
  4. Green House

 

Students are also divided into clubs-

  1. Art & Craft Club
  2. Health and Wellness Club
  3. Cultural Activity Club
  4. Eco Club etc.

 

These clubs and houses organize following activities

  • Physical Activities
  • Literary Activities
  • Social Activities
  • Aesthetic Activities
  • Cultural Activities
  • Moral Development Activities
  • Patriotic Activities
  • Multipurpose Activities
  • Academic Development Activities etc.
