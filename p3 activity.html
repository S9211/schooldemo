Activities and Curriculum

The students of Nursery to Grade-II are offered a variety of activities. However the activities become more diverse from Grade-III onwards. From Grade-III onwards the students are divided into houses-

  1. Red House
  2. Blue House
  3. Yellow House
  4. Green House

 

Students are also divided into clubs-

  1. Art & Craft Club
  2. Health and Wellness Club
  3. Cultural Activity Club
  4. Eco Club etc.

 

These clubs and houses organize following activities-

  • Physical Activities
  • Literary Activities
  • Social Activities
  • Aesthetic Activities
  • Cultural Activities
  • Moral Development Activities
  • Patriotic Activities
  • Multipurpose Activities
  • Academic Development Activities etc.


Curriculum is updated as per the instructions of CBSE, New Delhi every year. Curriculum prescripbed by CBSE is followed strictly which includes curricular as well as co-curricular activites.



Academic Calander- Every year session starts from 1st April.

 1. SESSION STARTS- 1st APRIL

 2. 1ST PERIODIC TEST- DURING THIRD WEEK OF MAY

 3. SUMMER VACATIONS - 1ST JUNE TO 30TH JUNE

 4. 2ND PERIODIC TEST- DURING LAST WEEK OF AUGUST

 5. MID TERM EXAM- DURING SECOND WEEK OF SEPTEMBER

 6. 3RD PERIODIC TEST- DURING THIRD WEEK OF NOVEMBER

 7. 4TH PERIODIC TEST- DURING THIRD WEEK OF JANUARY

 8. FINAL EXAM- DURING FIRST WEEK OF MARCH
