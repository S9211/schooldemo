ADMISSION


Admission is restricted by the policies of the Management and by condition of age and ability.

Age of admission Class wise

Pre Nursery - Below three years

Nursery - Three and above

K.G. - Four and Above

For Standard 1st with a corresponding increase in the higher standards on or before 31st March.

New admission to different class is subject to the vacancies in each class, in the month of April after the Annual Results are declared

Every new pupil must bring
1. Passport size photo
2. Photocopy of Aadhar Card
3. DOB certificate
4. School Leaving Certificate, if the pupil comes from a recognized school. Pupil applying for admission, are as a rule, subject to an entrance examination held at the beginning of the academic year. They will be tested in the standard below the one to which they seek admission. The Principal reserves the right of admission.

Books and Fee- Books prescribed to the students are mostly published by NCERT, but to enhance the knowledge of students some books of private publishers are also followed.

FEE charged from the students is fixed according to norms issued by state government and CBSE, New Delhi.
